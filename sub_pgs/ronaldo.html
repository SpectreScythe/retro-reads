'Everybody called Cristiano Ronaldo crazy but…': Neymar

Stating that he is looking forward to facing Ronaldo, the 31-year-old Brazil forward also said that he is excited to face the likes of Benzema, Firmino among others. Neymar has signed a two-year contract with the Riyadh-based club after moving from Paris St Germain.“I believe Cristiano Ronaldo started all of this and everybody called him ‘crazy’, and this and that.

“It is exciting, meeting top-quality players on the other teams thrills you and motivates you to play even better And it is a given when you face Ronaldo, Benzema, Firmino, that the excitement is even greater,I am very excited to write a new story with the club and my team mates ” he added.

Neymar & Cristiano Ronaldo are two of the greatest footballers of all time.They've both won numerous individual & team awards,and are considered to be among the most marketable athletes in the world.The Saudi Pro League has invested over 600 million euros to lure top players and coaches from European clubs.
