Oppenheimer vs Barbie: Who’s won the Barben-heimer war?

By Jame Gamble , Aug 15 2023

The biggest entertainment phenomenon of 2023 saw two films grossed a combined $244 million, the most successful opening weekend since "Avengers: End Game." Together, Barben-heimer was a massive success.

From Left - Margot Robbie as Barbie , Cilian Murphy as Oppernheimer

From Left - Margot Robbie as Barbie , Cilian Murphy as Oppernheimer

What is Barben-hemier?

Barbenheimer is the fan-created nickname for the simultaneous release of Greta Gerwig‘s Barbie and Christopher Nolan‘s Oppenheimer on July 21, 2023.



There are no similarities or overlaps between the two films except for their release date, though it’s safe to assume that neither Universal (which is producing Oppenheimer) nor Warner Bros. (which is producing Barbie) wanted to compete with Mission: Impossible — Dead Reckoning Part One , which debuted on July 12.

The ‘Barbenheimer’ phenomenon has taken the globe by storm as both ‘Barbie’ and ‘Oppenheimer’ have become two of the biggest and most discussed films of 2023



The double feature phenomenon was an undeniable, worldwide success.

Who Won?

Well, it turns out that more people opted to see “Barbie” and her journey of self discovery as the comedy opened with $155 million at the North American box office, Warner Bros. reported July 23.



With that lofty amount, Greta Gerwig’s “Barbie” broke multiple records, including the largest opening for a female-directed film and the largest domestic opening of 2023, passing “The Super Mario Bros. Movie.”3.

Fan Reactions

Leading up to the major box office weekend, “Barbenheimer” became a meme that overtook the internet about the world’s most famous doll battling the scientific mind behind the atomic bomb.



Fans shared their reactions to seeing “Barbie” and “Oppenheimer” all weekend long. One social media user posted a clip of their dramatic wardrobe change, as the poster finished Nolan’s movie and quickly prepared to enter Barbieland.
